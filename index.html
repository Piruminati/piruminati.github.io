INFINITE - Sprite Trainer Creator

Pokémon Trainer Creator is an online free trainer generator for your Pokémon games! Try it out!

What is INFINITE?

It is an application to help game creators and players themselves create graphics even without having much idea about image editing or pixel art.

How much does it cost?

It is totally FREE, the application does not require any payment, however the maintenance of the website is financed through advertising on this website and direct donations that you can make from here!

Infinite MiniSprite Creator

Inifinite MiniSprite Creator is a tool that will help you create your own overworlds for your Pokémon fangames. It has countless options.

Select between different types of clothing, hair, skin and accessories to create your characters in a matter of seconds.

When you finish creating, you can easily insert your creations into your projects created with RPG Maker XP or Unity.
